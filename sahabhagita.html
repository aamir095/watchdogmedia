Sahabhagita

This weekly Television programme a magazine format programme which is broadcasted via Sagarmatha Television on every Saturday at 8:30 PM. It primarily focuses on emerging development and public concerned issues of post earthquake. Awareness, education, sensitization and advocacy are some of the core issues that we place with priority in the programme.
