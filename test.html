Welcome to Watchdog Media Services

Watchdog Media Services Pvt. Ltd. (WMS) is an organization established by an enthusiastic group of young and dynamic development journalists with the aim to contribute in the process of social transformation through development journalism.

  • Ten years of experience
  • Training to media professionals
  • IEC/BCC materials designing and Production
  • Broad nettelevision with different stakeholders
  • Media research
  • Documentary Production, Radio Program Production & PSA/Jingle/TVC Production
  • Production of three weekly Television Programmess and their dissemination via National Television Channels
